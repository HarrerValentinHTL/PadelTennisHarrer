Padel Tennis

Padel tennis is a racket sport that combines elements of tennis and squash, typically played in doubles on a smaller court surrounded by walls. The game uses solid, stringless rackets and balls that are slightly softer than tennis balls. Padel is easy to learn, social, and has gained global popularity since originating in Mexico.
