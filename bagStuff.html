Stuff to have in a bag for Padel

  • Padel Racket: Essential for play, tailored to your skill level and style.
  • Padel Balls: A tube of Padel balls to ensure you have enough for play and replacements.
  • Water Bottle: Staying hydrated is vital for performance and health.
  • Sports Towel: For drying sweat during breaks and after the game.
  • Extra T-Shirt: To change into post-game or if you sweat through the first.
  • Extra Socks: An extra pair in case the initial ones become uncomfortable due to sweat.
  • Padel Shoes: Specific footwear designed for the sport, offering proper support and traction.
  • Sunscreen: Protect your skin during outdoor play.
  • First Aid Kit: Basic supplies for minor injuries or ailments, including band-aids and antiseptic wipes.
  • Deodorant: To stay fresh before and after your games.
  • Cap or Visor: Shields your eyes and face from the sun in outdoor settings.
